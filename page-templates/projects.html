Main projects

You'll find below the projects I've been working on (and the main reason I only spent max 20h of work on that one woopsy). Note that some of them are still in progress, some coming out soon and some others in early dev.

Graphitech

Established in Yamachiche since 2001, Graphitech's mission is to offer various advertising services including lettering and graphic design. They are constantly innovating with various complementary services in order to meet the needs of their customers. Their experienced team of graphic designers, computer graphic designers and technical installers will surely meet your expectations in terms of impact and visibility.

La Pousse Santé

La Pousse Santé is a local healthy market providing Louiseville with many goodies, their talented team of cooks and pastry cooks prepare a variety of homemade products with comforting flavours every day. To try them is to adopt them!

AQCDI

The “Association québécoise des centres d’intervention en dependence” (AQCID) is a national association regrouping more than hundred community and private organizations working in addiction. The AQCID is committed to be an interlocutor and a partner of the public network for services delivery in addiction to the population and to offer high quality services.

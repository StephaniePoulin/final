Motivations & Inovations!

You'll find below the type of work I look foward to make and a quick description of why. Feel free to take a look on their website and support them!

Outlab

Outlab is a portfolio website. What amaze me are the cool navigation features such as the transition between page and the whole continuity of it. They're taking the word "navigation" to an whole new level!

Cinelux

Cinelux is a also portfolio websit, but what amaze me is the whole light effect of their page. It makes it more lively for those navigating throughout their page.

Blindsight

Blindsight is a page presenting a short film. The thing that inspired me first was the cursor animation. Animating the cursor has more impact in my opinion than hover animation or so if used correctly. You should also check out their "Memories" page, you'll know instantly why I fell in love with the website

Cobo

If you're using a mac, your fans are probably already kicking in while navigating this web page, but you'll agree it's worth it. Cobo is basically a website for a molding enterprise but the 3D effect on this page is simply neat.

Ugly drinks

You probably guessed it, but Ugly drinks is a website for the Ugly drinks product. Although it made it's spot here for the design, this website is simply enjoyable. It marvelously integrate user-friendliness we want for a website while still having some pretty decent animation to it!
